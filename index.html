About

Hi! I'm Bridget Kuzma

Born and raised in Kensington, MD I studied psychology and painting in Texas at the University of Dallas. I have been inspired by my experience working at the National Institutes of Health among children with serious medical conditions. After 10 years of involvement with this rich challenging environment I moved to Austin for reflection, exploration and ART!

A variety of creative roles & unique collaborations there led me to Brooklyn where I am pursuing a masters in Creative Arts Therapy at The Pratt Institute.

Central themes of my work include mindfulness and rediscovery, in nature as well as our built environment. While always experimenting with new materials including found objects, my primary mediums are oils & acrylics. Perpetual curiosity leads me to diverse subjects and I am always interested in challenging commissions involving new territory.
